Frequently asked questions

Do I need Web3 or crypto experience?

Not for most roles. We hire for craft and mindset. Familiarity with Web3 is a plus for specific roles — it's noted on the listing when relevant.

Can I apply to multiple roles at once?

Yes. If you're a strong fit for more than one position, apply to each separately with a short note explaining your interest in both.

Are all roles on-site, or is remote available?

It depends on the role. Each listing is clearly tagged — On-site, Hybrid, or Remote. Most engineering roles offer hybrid flexibility.

What should I prepare before applying?

Portfolio or GitHub if relevant, your updated CV, and a short note on why this role interests you. No cover letter required.

How long does the full process take?

Typically 2–3 weeks from first contact to offer. Urgent roles may move faster — sometimes within 1 week.

Will I hear back if I'm not selected?

Yes. We send a clear update to every applicant whether or not we're moving forward. We don't leave people waiting indefinitely.

There's no role that fits me right now — what can I do?

Share your profile via the form below. We keep strong candidates on file and reach out when a matching role opens up.

Do you support relocation?

We support relocation assistance and work permits for Senior and above roles on a case-by-case basis. Raise it during your intro call.
